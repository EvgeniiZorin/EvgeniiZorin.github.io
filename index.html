About me

Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Evgenii Zorin and I am an avid Data Science practitioner. The thing I do most often in my work is clean and organise large amounts of my clients' data, then use various visualisation and storytelling techniques to present the findings. My goal is then to convey the key insights that I found in the data to my clients in a concise and accessible manner.

My work experience includes performing many tasks related to Data Science and Programming:

  • Building and hosting interactive Dashboards with different tools - Python libraries (Streamlit, Plotly Dash) as well as Excel and Google Sheets;
  • Making beautiful data visualisations upon the customer's requests and needs;
  • Consulting my clients on the use of Artificial Intelligence for their use case - not everything needs Machine Learning or Generative AI!
  • Writing programs in Python for various tasks, for example, performing mathematical simulations, automating routine tasks, etc.
